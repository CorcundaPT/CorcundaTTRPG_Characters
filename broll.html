Rage (4 uses)

On your turn, you can enter Rage as a Bonus Action.

While Raging, you gain the following benefits if you aren't wearing Heavy Armor:

  • Advantage on Strength checks and Strength Saving Throws.
  • +2 Melee weapon Damage rolls.
  • Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing Damage.

You can't cast spells neither concentrate on them while raging.

The Rage lasts for 1 minute. It ends early if you are knocked Unconscious or if you haven't taken nor dealt damage since last turn. You can also end your Rage as a Bonus Action.

After a long rest, you regain all Rage uses.
